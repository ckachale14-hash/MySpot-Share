This policy governs all advertising on MySpot Share — boosted posts and campaigns
created via the Ads Manager. **All ads are reviewed before they serve** and must
follow both this policy and the [Community Guidelines](./COMMUNITY_GUIDELINES.md).
By submitting an ad you confirm it complies and that you have the rights to its
content.

## 1. Who can advertise
Advertisers must have a valid account in good standing, accurately represent who
they are and what they're promoting, and comply with all applicable laws in the
markets they target.

## 2. Review process
Campaigns are submitted, **funded, and then queued for review**. We approve, reject,
or request changes before delivery, and we may pause or remove a live ad at any time
if it breaks this policy. Approval is not an endorsement and does not guarantee
performance.

## 3. Prohibited ad content
Ads must not promote or contain:
- Anything that violates the Community Guidelines (scams, fraud, hate, etc.).
- **Deceptive financial promotions:** guaranteed returns, "get rich quick",
  Ponzi/pyramid/MLM deception, or unlicensed investment/securities solicitation.
- Illegal products or services; counterfeit or stolen goods.
- False, misleading, or unsubstantiated claims, or "before/after" results that
  can't be evidenced.
- Sexually explicit content, shock content, or content exploiting tragedies.
- Discriminatory promotion or targeting based on protected characteristics
  (e.g. in housing, employment, or credit offers).
- Malware, deceptive "you won" mechanics, or misleading buttons/clickbait.
- Infringement of others' intellectual property or privacy.

## 4. Restricted categories (allowed only with compliance)
These require applicable licenses, disclosures, and legal compliance, and may be
limited by market and audience:
- Financial services, lending, and **crypto/digital assets** (clear risk
  disclosures; no guaranteed returns).
- Health, supplements, and medical claims (evidence‑based; no miracle cures).
- Alcohol, gambling/betting, and other age‑restricted goods (age‑gated where
  required; prohibited where not permitted).
- Political or social‑issue ads (transparency and local‑law compliance).

## 5. Quality and transparency
- Ads are clearly labeled (e.g. **"Sponsored"**).
- Creative must be accurate and match the landing experience; no broken,
  deceptive, or unexpected destinations.
- Reasonable text/quality standards; no excessive or distorted claims.

## 6. Targeting and data
Target audiences must be lawful and non‑discriminatory. Don't use ads to collect
personal data deceptively, and honor our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md) and
applicable data‑protection law.

## 7. Billing
Ad spend is prepaid/charged through the in‑app advertising flow and metered against
delivery. Amounts already delivered are non‑refundable except where required by law
or our discretion; rejected or undelivered budget is handled per our refund
practices.

## 8. Enforcement
We may reject, pause, or remove ads and campaigns, and restrict advertising
privileges, for violations — with notice where practicable. Repeated or serious
violations may lead to account action under the Terms.

## 9. Changes
We will update this policy as needed and apply the current version to new and
running campaigns.
