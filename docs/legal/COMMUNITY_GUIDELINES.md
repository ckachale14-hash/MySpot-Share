MySpot Share exists to help entrepreneurs, business owners, customers,
professionals, and investors **connect, learn, and grow** — and to share honest
Founder Journeys: the real story of how a business started, the capital, the
mistakes, and the lessons. These guidelines keep that community trustworthy. They
apply to everything on the platform: posts, articles, Founder Journeys, polls,
comments, stories, business listings, reviews, live streams, messages, profiles,
and ads.

## The short version
Be real. Be respectful. Don't scam people. Don't spam. Keep people safe.

## 1. Be authentic
- Use a real identity or a clearly identified business; don't impersonate people,
  brands, or organizations.
- Founder Journeys and business claims should be truthful. Don't fabricate
  results, funding, or credentials.
- No fake accounts, bought followers, or coordinated inauthentic behavior.

## 2. Be respectful
- No harassment, bullying, threats, or targeted abuse.
- No hate speech or attacks based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex,
  gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or similar protected traits.
- Disagree with ideas, not by demeaning people.

## 3. No scams, fraud, or deceptive money‑making
This is a business community, so we are strict here:
- No Ponzi/pyramid schemes, "money‑doubling", advance‑fee fraud, or deceptive MLM.
- No fake investment opportunities, guaranteed‑return promises, or unlicensed
  financial/securities solicitation.
- No phishing, account‑takeover attempts, or payment fraud.
- Be transparent about paid promotions and affiliate relationships.

## 4. No spam or platform manipulation
- No repetitive, irrelevant, or bulk content; no engagement‑baiting or
  follow/unfollow churn.
- Don't manipulate rankings, reviews, votes, or metrics.
- Reviews must reflect genuine experience; no incentivized or fake reviews.

## 5. Keep people safe
- Zero tolerance for content that sexually exploits or endangers minors. We remove
  it and report it to the authorities.
- No content that promotes, incites, or threatens violence, terrorism, or harm.
- No sale of illegal or dangerous goods (weapons, drugs, stolen data, etc.).
- No graphic, gratuitously violent, or non‑consensual intimate content.

## 6. Respect privacy and IP
- Don't post others' private or identifying information without consent ("doxxing").
- Only share content you have the rights to; respect trademarks and copyrights.

## 7. Integrity of information
- Don't post harmful misinformation, especially deceptive financial or health
  claims. Clearly label AI‑generated media where it could mislead.

## 8. Reporting and your tools
Everyone helps keep MySpot healthy. You can **report** posts, accounts, businesses,
and messages, and **block** accounts, directly in the app. Reports are reviewed by
our moderation team and automated systems.

## 9. Enforcement
Depending on severity and history, we may: remove content, limit its reach, apply a
warning, restrict features, suspend, or permanently ban an account, and where
required report to authorities. We consider context and may make exceptions for
clear educational, documentary, or newsworthy value.

## 10. Appeals
If you believe we made a mistake, you may appeal via {{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}. We aim to
review appeals promptly and will restore content or access where warranted.

These guidelines evolve with the community; we'll update them as needed.
