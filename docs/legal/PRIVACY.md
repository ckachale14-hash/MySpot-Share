## 1. Information we collect

**You give us:**
- **Account & profile:** display name, unique @handle, email and/or phone number
  (used for sign‑in), and optional photo, bio, industry, account type, and links.
- **Identity/verification documents** (only if you apply for a verified badge):
  government ID and, for businesses, registration documents.
- **Content:** posts, Founder Journeys, comments, poll votes, stories, business
  listings and reviews, live‑stream content, and direct/group messages.
- **Payment details:** processed by our payment partners (we do **not** store full
  card numbers); we keep a transaction record (amount, currency, status, purpose).

**Collected automatically:**
- **Device & usage:** app interactions, screens viewed, approximate
  device/OS info, and a push‑notification token.
- **Diagnostics:** crash reports and performance metrics.

**From third parties:** authentication confirmations from Google/Apple/your phone
carrier when you use those sign‑in methods.

## 2. How we use your information

- Provide and operate the platform (feeds, profiles, messaging, live, search).
- Authenticate you and secure accounts (including App Check and abuse prevention).
- Power AI features you invoke (content assistance, image/video generation) and
  **content moderation** (see §5).
- Process payments for subscriptions, verification, and advertising.
- Send notifications you've enabled (you control categories in Settings).
- Analytics to understand and improve the product; diagnostics to fix crashes.
- Comply with law and enforce our Terms and community standards.

## 3. Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

Performance of a contract (operating your account); legitimate interests (safety,
fraud prevention, product improvement); consent (push notifications, optional
features); and legal obligation (tax/financial records, lawful requests).

## 4. Who we share with (sub‑processors)

We do **not** sell your personal data. We share it with service providers acting
on our behalf:

| Provider | Purpose | Data involved |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| Google **Firebase** (Auth, Firestore, Storage, Functions, Messaging, App Check, Crashlytics, Analytics, Performance) | Core backend, hosting, auth, push, diagnostics | Account, content, device, diagnostics |

| **Paystack / Flutterwave** (web) | Payment processing in Africa | Payment and contact details |
| **RevenueCat** + **Apple App Store / Google Play** (mobile) | In‑app purchases | Purchase/subscription status |
| {{SEARCH_PROVIDER, e.g. Algolia}} | Search indexing (if enabled) | Public profile/post fields |

We may also disclose information to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect
the rights and safety of users and the public.

## 5. AI features and content moderation

Some features send your content to AI providers (currently **OpenAI**) to generate
suggestions, images, or video, and to **automatically screen content** for safety.
AI outputs may be inaccurate — you are responsible for what you publish. We do not
use your private messages to train third‑party models, and our providers are bound
by their own data‑processing terms.

## 6. Payments

Payments are handled by the providers in §4. We retain transaction records
(not full card data) for accounting, tax, fraud‑prevention, and dispute purposes,
which may require keeping them after account deletion as permitted by law.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

We keep personal data while your account is active. **You can delete your account**
in *Settings → Delete account*. Deletion runs an automated cleanup that removes your
profile, posts, Founder Journeys, stories, businesses, @handle, and follow
relationships. Limited records (e.g. payment/transaction history, and content
others may have copied or that we must keep for legal/safety reasons) may be
retained as required by law. Backups are purged on a rolling schedule.

## 8. Your rights

Subject to your local law, you may request to **access, correct, export, restrict,
or delete** your personal data, **object** to certain processing, and **withdraw
consent**. EEA/UK users may complain to a supervisory authority; California users
have CCPA/CPRA rights (we do not sell or "share" data for cross‑context behavioral
advertising). Contact **{{PRIVACY_EMAIL}}**; we may verify your identity first.

## 9. Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Access to your data is constrained by Firebase
Security Rules, server‑only writes for sensitive fields (identity, payments,
ranking), and App Check on client calls. No system is perfectly secure; please use
a strong, unique credential and keep your device secure.

## 10. International transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than yours (including the United
States, where several providers operate). Where required, transfers rely on
appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.
