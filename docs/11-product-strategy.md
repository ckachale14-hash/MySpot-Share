# 11 · Product Strategy

> **MySpot Share — "Where Entrepreneurs Connect and Grow."**
> The leading entrepreneurship & business-networking ecosystem for **Africa first**,
> then global.

## 1. Vision

Make starting and growing a business **less lonely and less mysterious** in
Africa. Every entrepreneur learns from the real, unfiltered journeys of others —
capital, mistakes, lessons, milestones — and finds the customers, partners,
investors, and knowledge to grow. MySpot is where the next generation of African
founders is discovered, funded, followed, and inspired.

## 2. Competitive advantage (the moat)

| Advantage | Why it's defensible |
|-----------|--------------------|
| **Founder-Journey is the core object** | No major platform makes "how I started — capital, mistakes, lessons" a first-class, structured, discoverable unit. This content is unique, evergreen, SEO-rich, and emotionally sticky. |
| **Africa-first by design** | Mobile-money payments, **data-light** mode, Android-first, offline-first, local languages, phone-number identity. Western platforms treat Africa as an afterthought. |
| **Business + Social + AI in one** | LinkedIn's trust, Instagram/TikTok's engagement, plus AI tools that help under-resourced founders write, market, and learn — bundled for entrepreneurs specifically. |
| **Trust layer** | Verified businesses + reviews + ratings make commerce and networking safe — a real pain in informal-economy markets. |
| **Community + learning loop** | Live lessons, journeys, and creator-educators turn the network into a continuous business school. |

**Versus alternatives:** LinkedIn (corporate, weak African SME penetration,
no storytelling/commerce), Facebook/Instagram (not business-focused, no
verification/ads-for-SMEs tooling tuned to entrepreneurs), WhatsApp (1:1, no
discovery/feed/monetization), local classifieds (no social graph or content).

## 4. Monetization (summary)

Verification fees · premium memberships · sponsored ads · featured business
listings · AI credits — full design and **app-store compliance** in
[07 Monetization](07-monetization.md). Pricing is **localized per market** and
collected primarily via **mobile money** (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel) and cards.

## 5. Growth strategy

- **Content viral loop:** founder journeys are inherently shareable and
  SEO-indexable; "share to WhatsApp" is the primary distribution act in Africa.
- **Referral program:** Branch deferred deep links + rewards (premium days, AI
  credits) for inviting active users; track in `referrals/{code}`.
- **Creator/ambassador program:** recruit micro-influencer entrepreneurs &
  business educators; campus reps at universities/incubators.
- **Institutional partnerships:** business associations, SACCOs/cooperatives,
  incubators/accelerators, banks (SME programs), and **telcos** (data bundles,
  carrier billing, possible zero-rating of the lite app).
- **Vernacular & local relevance:** localized languages and locally-famous
  founder stories per market drive resonance.
- **Offline → online:** sponsor/host entrepreneurship meetups and live events
  that stream on MySpot.

## 6. User-acquisition strategy

| Channel | Tactic |
|---------|--------|
| Organic | SEO on public journeys/business pages; share-to-WhatsApp/Facebook; ASO |
| Referral | incentivized invites (Branch attribution) |
| Influencer | micro-entrepreneur creators, business coaches |
| Campus / community | ambassador reps, incubator partnerships |
| Telco | bundled data / carrier billing / co-marketing |
| PR & events | local startup media, founder-story features, meetups |
| Paid (later) | targeted Meta/Google/TikTok to lookalikes once retention is proven |

**Sequencing:** nail **retention** (a great feed + journeys + messaging) before
spending on paid acquisition. Track D1/D7/D30 and the founder-journey creation
rate as leading indicators ([07 §7 KPIs](07-monetization.md#7-kpis-to-instrument-from-day-one)).

## 7. North-star metric

**Weekly Active Entrepreneurs who engage with business content** (post, journey,
message, or live) — captures the real value loop, not vanity installs.
